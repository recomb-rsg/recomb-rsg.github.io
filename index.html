The Meeting

RECOMB-RSG 2026 is a key forum for advances in regulatory genomics and systems biology.

As a satellite meeting of RECOMB, we bring together a focused community of researchers to explore the algorithms, mathematical models, and machine learning techniques decoding the regulatory logic of the genome.
