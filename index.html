The Meeting

RECOMB-RSG 2026 is a key forum for advances in regulatory genomics and systems biology.

As a satellite meeting of RECOMB, we bring together a focused community of researchers to explore the algorithms, mathematical models, and machine learning techniques decoding the regulatory logic of the genome.

Call for Contributions

We invite submissions for oral presentations and posters in the following tracks.

Proceedings Track

For original, unpublished research offering significant contributions to the field.

  • 2 page abstract
  • Presentation of pre-print manuscripts

Highlights Track

For high-impact work already published in top-tier journals (Jan 2025 – Present).

  • 2 page abstract
  • Presentation of recent breakthrough findings

Poster Track

For late-breaking results, ongoing research, and preliminary findings.

  • 1 page abstract
  • Dedicated poster session
